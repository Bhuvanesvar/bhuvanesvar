ABOUT THE COLLEGE

The Universal College of Engineering &Technology is affiliated to JNTU Kakinada, approved by AICTE, New Delhi and also Accredited by NAAC with B++. Its campus tucked in a serene lush green and tranquil ambience at Dokiparru, Medikondur, Guntur in Andhra Pradesh. The campus spreads over 10.34 acres of picturesque land scape and it's an ideal and congenial place for Academic Excellence. UCET offers Seven Under Graduate courses, B.Tech in Civil Engineering, EEE, Mechanical Engineering, ECE and CSE, Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning, CSE(Data Science). The college also offers Three Post Graduate courses with M. Tech in Structural Engineering and CSE along with MBA. The college is furnished with highly sophisticated state of the art laboratories, fully ventilated classrooms, hygienic canteen, indoor & out door playgrounds to make the students fulfil their intellectual pursuits and to equip themselves with all round development.
